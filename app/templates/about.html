About Us

Rapid Axis is a family owned and run business that is dedicated to developing long term relationships in the Mechanical Engineering and Custom Component Buying community throughout the by providing quality rapid manufacturing and prototyping services at a low cost. Our leadership is formed by two generations of Machinists.

We believe that through a combination of in-house capabilities and carefully selected partners we can provide quick prototyping and production services without sacrificing either cost or quality.
